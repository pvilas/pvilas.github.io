--- layout: post title: Django name field on auth_permission too short date: '2013-07-10T18:09:00.001+02:00' author: Pere Vilas tags: - django modified_time: '2013-07-10T18:10:04.102+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-9191328551308072706.post-8488115373112852287 blogger_orig_url: http://www.pvilas.com/2013/07/django-name-field-on-authpermission-too.html --- If the verbose_name of the table is over 80 characters the syncdb command will silently fail.
The name field on auth_permissions table is too short to store if verbose_name are that long, so it doesn't update and you cannot assign permissions.
The obvious workaround is to manually change the field length on the table.
